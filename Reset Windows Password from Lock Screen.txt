01. Press & Hold "Shift" then "Restart" the pc.
02. Click on "TroubleShoot > Advanced Option > Command Prompt" .
03. Type and click "Enter" after each line :	
	1. C:
	2. cd windows
	3. cd system32
	4. ren utilman.exe utilman1.exe
	5. ren cmd.exe utilman.exe
	6. exit
04. after Clicking "Enter" Click "Continue" and wait for PC to "Restart".
05. After Restart > Click on "Accessablity Icon" > "CMD" will open.
06. Type "control userpasswords2" > click "Enter". "User Account Panel" will Open.
07. From There "select your User" > click "Reset Password" > and "Set New Password" and "click Enter".
08. Now to Reset the "Accessablity Button".
09. "Repet Step-1 & Step-2" then "Step-3 Command Changes"
10. Type and click "Enter" after each line :	
	1. C:
	2. cd windows
	3. cd system32
	4. ren utilman.exe cmd.exe
	5. ren utilman1.exe utilman.exe 
	6. exit
11. PC will Restart and Accessablity will change back just as it was before.
......... Now can "Login" with your "New Password" .........
