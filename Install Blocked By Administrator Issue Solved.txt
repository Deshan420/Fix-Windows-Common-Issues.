1. Search "Registry Editor" and run as Administrator.
2. HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE> SOFTWER> MICROSOFT> WINDOWS> CurrentVersion> Policies> System
3. Select "EnableLUA" & double click on it.
4. Replace Value "1" with "0" & Click "OK".
5. Close the Window & Restart your PC.
